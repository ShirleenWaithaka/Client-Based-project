What We Offer

DEBT RECOVERY
Our expertise is in debt recovery for companies with delinquent accounts. We handle both retail and corporate collections through client meetings, demand letters, calls and legal support. Our approach is customized based on client profiles, utilizing field collection teams and call center support to ensure high contact rates and effective results.

INVESTIGATION/SKIP TRACING
Due to diversity in our underlying client base and continous experience in field based collections, we are able to easily carry out investigations to trace debtors whom you have lost contact with. This also includes serving legal and statutory notices.

PROCESS SERVER
Our experiences, processes and court servers provide a proffesional and time effective service of documents

ASSET SEARCHES
Our dedicated team of investigators are trained in asset searching, with the knowledge of various public information databases.

DEBT SURVEILLANCE
We undertake debt surveillance and monitoring on debts that may have been written by companies.Upon confirmation of changes in the customers financial circumstances, we duly advise our clients.

CONSULTANCY
We are consultants on:
- Setting us credit policies and system strategies.
- Training on debt collection.

RECONCILIATION
Once you furnish us with data, we work in liason with your customer, establishing actual amounts before collection.
